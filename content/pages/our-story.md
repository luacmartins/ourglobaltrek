## Our Current Trek

We are in the midst of what we're fondly referring to as ["The Great Expedition"](/travel-blog/embarking-great-expedition/) - the trip of our dreams to many bucket list destinations, criss-crossing the world. So far, it's been a wild ride!

The highlight has been our top destination - [Antarctica](/travel-blog/antarctica/) (!!!) - during which we:

- Took a [dream 3-week cruise](/travel-blog/22-day-ultimate-antarctica-expedition/) through the [Falkland Islands](/travel-blog/falkland-islands/), [South Georgia](/travel-blog/south-georgia/), and the [Antarctica Peninsula](/travel-blog/antarctica-frozen-continent/)
- Saw more than [a million penguins](/travel-blog/south-georgia/) of all different species total
- Survived the [polar plunge in a snowstorm](/travel-blog/antarctica-frozen-continent/) and near freezing water

"The Great Expedition" began in Africa, where we:

- Went on [safari in Kenya](/travel-blog/safari-in-kenya/) as well as [safari in Tanzania](/travel-blog/safari-in-tanzania/) in incredible places like the Masai Mara and the Serengeti
- Perched on the edge of [Victoria Falls](/travel-blog/victoria-falls/), looking down the massive waterfall in Zambia
- Soaked up the sun in the [paradise of the Seychelles](/travel-blog/seychelles-beach-paradise/)
- Travelled [overland by truck](/travel-blog/22-days-overland-absolute-africa/) through Zimbabwe, Botswana, Namibia and South Africa

Then in South America, we:

- Marvelled at the remote and mysterious [moai](/travel-blog/chile-easter-island/) in [Easter Island](/travel-blog/easter-island-moai/)
- Hiked the [Torres del Paine "W Trek"](/travel-blog/torres-del-paine-hiking-w-trek/) over 5 days
- [Beach-hopped in southern Brazil](/travel-blog/brazils-southern-beaches/), where Carlos grew up
- Came face-to-face with the massive [Perito Moreno glacier](/travel-blog/el-calafate-perito-moreno-glacier/) in Patagonia

After a brief interlude in [Portugal](/travel-blog/portugal/), our adventures continued. We then:

- Met up with our friends and [rang in the new year in Morocco](/travel-blog/morocco-travel-friends/)
- Completed the ultimate [pilgrimage in the Holy Land in Israel](/travel-blog/israel-old-meets-new/)
- Awed over [Jordan's historic and natural wonders](/travel-blog/jordan/) (Petra!)
- Finally saw the [ancient civilizations in Egypt](/travel-blog/egypt-ancient-history/)

Visiting all seven continent was achieved in Oceania, where we:

- Frolicked in [idyllic islands in Fiji](/travel-blog/bula-fiji/)
- Lived [#vanlife](/travel-blog/vanlife-south-island-nz/) for two weeks in South Island New Zealand
- Toured overwater bungalows and petted stingrays in the true [paradise of French Polynesia](/travel-blog/french-polynesia-pearl-of-the-pacific/)

Keep following us for the next adventure!
