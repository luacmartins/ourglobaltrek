As regional experts in Asia, we are often approached for advice for where to go for an Asian vacation. The way we would answer that question would be to ask what kind of vacation one is looking for. A tropical beach getaway? An exotic cultural experience? An exhilarating adventure through the wild? It’s impossible to pick one favourite place during our Asian travels, but we know our favourites in every category.

Below we present what we considered the best of Asia destinations in each of the categories.

## City

The most exciting cities were bustling urban centers filled with sprawling skyscrapers, megamalls, endless people, and always something to do.

**Winner:** [Hong Kong](/travel-blog/3-ways-enjoy-hong-kong-skyline/ "3 Ways to Enjoy the Hong Kong Skyline")
