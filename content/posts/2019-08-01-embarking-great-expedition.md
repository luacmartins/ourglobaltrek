We are beyond excited to share that we'll be headed on the trip of our dreams, which we're unofficially dubbing "The Great Expedition!"

While our [Asia](/travel-blog/asia-year-in-review/) and [South America](/travel-blog/category/country/south-america/) trips were unforgettable regional explorations, this time we'll be truly criss-crossing the globe, including many long-time bucket list items. Travel is a lifelong hobby of ours and it's hard to describe just how much we're looking forward to this one.

## Destinations

All 7 continents, here we come! We're especially psyched for Antarctica, Africa, and Oceania, which are new ones for us. More importantly, we're going for experiences all around the world we've wanted to hit for a while - safaris, hikes, cruises, drives, you name it. The list of countries in the plans:

![travel itinerary ](images/map-1440x842.png)

AFRICA - Kenya, Tanzania, Seychelles, Zimbabwe, Zambia, Botswana, Namibia, South Africa, Morocco, Egypt

ANTARCTICA - including Falkland islands and South Georgia

ASIA - Jordan, Israel, Turkey

EUROPE - Germany, Portugal, Turkey

NORTH AMERICA - Canada, USA

OCEANIA - Australia, New Zealand, Fiji, French Polynesia

SOUTH AMERICA - Brazil, Chile, Argentina

Any and all recommendations for the above welcome!

## Blogging

We started this blog because of the last long term trip we took in Asia. It wasn't always easy but we have absolutely loved having such a detailed record of our thoughts during that period of time to share. However, this time we'll be taking a different approach - more photography and story-based, rather than location and topic-based. Everyone likes the pictures the most anyway. :)

Thanks to everyone for your love and support of all of our adventures over the years - it has meant the world to us. <3

Much more to come.
