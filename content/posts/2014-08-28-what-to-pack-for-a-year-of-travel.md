What to pack is an essential and popular topic among travelers - especially backpackers, since everything you own will literally be in a bag on your back. It’s also a bit crazy and overwhelming to imagine packing for an entire year! We slowly gathered our list of items over the course of a few months. Packing lists out there on the web helped, but at the end, it came down to what we thought we would realistically use on the road. There are tons of cool gadgets out there designed for every aspect of travel; however, if we’re not going to use it regularly, it’s not worth bringing.

Here is the complete list of items we packed into our backpacks split by category:
